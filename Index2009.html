Why people say gates of hell is because everything turned from light to dark very quickly - the sun got blocked out by the smoke. The only thing you could see is the glow of the fire through the smoke. We were choking. We only had large tea towels which we were wetting down constantly and wrapping around our faces so we could breathe.
